## How to Use

### **Step-by-Step:**

1. **Select Data Source**
   - Choose: Google Places, Foursquare, or Yelp

2. **Select State** (Required)
   - Choose from 50 US states
   - Cities will automatically populate based on your state selection

3. **Select City** (Required)
   - After selecting a state, the city dropdown becomes active
   - Shows 20-24 major cities for each state

4. **Select Business Category** (Required)
   - Choose from 15 pre-configured home service categories
   - Or enter a custom category below

5. **Click "Search Businesses"**
   - Results will show up to 50 businesses

6. **Export to CSV**
   - Download your results for cold calling
